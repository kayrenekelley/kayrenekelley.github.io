Cycle the Continental Divide

The Contental Divide event is held once a year. This year it will be held July 4th. It is a cross country race that covers 30 miles. It will start at 7:00am in the morning.

This years course will cover 30 miles both flat and mountainous roads. The course will be marked out the day before and will not be published until registration.

Registration will take place at the rodeo grounds and start at 6:00am, first come first serve. Entry fee is $20 per person. There will be check points and water stations. Pre-registration may be done on this page.
